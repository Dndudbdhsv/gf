About goldfish


The goldfish is a freshwather fish. It is one of the most commonly kept aquarium fish.


Size

The largest goldfish in the world was belived by the BBC to measure 19 inches (48 cm), and be living in the Netherlands.


Food

A good-food quality varienty of goldfish flaskes will be suitable. But do be careful of over-feeding; make sure there aren't uneaten flakes left floatng in the water after a little while, as these can go off, harming your fish in the process.


Lifespan

Do you know that with the proper enviroment and care, pet goldfish can acctually live for decades?
